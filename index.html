How to order

You may get in touch with my contact page. Please fill the form in the contact section providing data such as name, address, portrait size.

Mediums Used

  • Graphite pencils
  • Charcoal pencils
  • Paper with thickness 200gsm

Based on your request, I'll reach you out as soon as possible along with the timeframe.

Here I am providing the sizes of the portraits and an example image of related sizes.

Paper Sizes

  • A3 - 16.5 x 11.7 inches - 29.7 x 42 cm
  • A4 - 8.27 x 11.69 inches - 21 x 29.7 cm
  • A5 - 5.83 x 8.27 inches - 14.81 x 21.01 cm

Photo requirements & tips

I use clarity pictures to obtain more objective value and the detailing. Therefore choose a picture that you feel best describes the subject's, looks and personality. For a portrait of more than one person you can send seperate pictures. However I recomend both shoud have same view points.
